I'm Aubrey, or Foxintale in most places. I've been doing avatar work in Unity and Blender for a little under two years, and I've done texture work since mid 2020 across four different games. (MSFS 2020, Subnautica, Skyrim SE and VRC)
While I specialise in realistic looking fur textures, I can also do flat textures, custom clothing textures, and some solid objects such as props, or world items.

General quotes are free, and the timeframe I aim for is two to four weeks.
I DO NOT do "Last minute" / "I have an event tomorrow" rush jobs. That is way too stressful. Find someone else.

Finally, if the site looks wonky kindly turn your device into landscape mode. I coded it myself so it isn't perfect. I'm still working on mobile compatibility in portrait mode..
