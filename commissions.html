I'm Aubrey, or Foxintale in most places. I've done texture work for three years across four different games (Subnautica, MSFS 2020, Skyrim and VRC).
While I specialise in realistic looking fur textures, I can also do custom clothing textures, and some solid objects such as props, or world items.
