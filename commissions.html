I'm Aubrey, or Foxintale in most places. I've done texture work for three years across four different games (Subnautica, MSFS 2020, Skyrim and VRC).
While I specialise in realistic looking fur textures, I can also do custom clothing textures, and some solid objects such as props, or world items.

Pricing

  • A "+" indicates it goes up based on complexity. We'll discuss once contacted.
  • All prices assume I have the asset already, otherwise you add the cost of the asset for both of us.

  • Retextures:

  • Avatar retexture (Flat colours): $20+
  • Avatar retexture (Fur brushed or detailed): $30+
  • Clothing retexture (If I have the asset already): $10+
  • Prop retextures (Same applies to clothing): $5+

  • Unity Related:

  • PC only project setup: $15+
  • Quest only project setup: $10+
  • PC + Quest project setup: $20+
  • Simple on/off toggles: Up to 30 toggles free, then $0.5/per additional.
  • Texture optimization: Free.
  • Animator optimisation (Direct Blend Tree): Free.
