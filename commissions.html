I'm Aubrey, or Foxintale in most places. I've done texture work for three years across four different games. (MSFS 2020, Subnautica, Skyrim SE and VRC)
While I specialise in realistic looking fur textures, I can also do flat textures, custom clothing textures, and some solid objects such as props, or world items.

Commission cost quotes are free, and the timeframe I aim for is within a week or two once commissioned.

Finally, if the site looks wonky kindly turn your device into landscape mode. I coded it myself so it isn't perfect.

Commission Info:

  • Contact me over Discord at "foxintale". Please don't just say "hi" or "hello" I won't respond. Nothing personal, it's annoying.
  • I aim for within two weeks, but do not hold me to that. In addition to working a full time job in retail, I may take periodic breaks of a day or two to avoid burnout. A month is the absolute longest it will take.
  • If you want a base re-textured that I do not have, if you purchase it for me then the commission price is halved or free depending on the cost of the base.
  • Once comissioned, if you like I can livestream progress via Discord DMs if you would like.
    No charge for this, you only need to let me know that you would like this to be done.
  • If you would like something listed as free, just let me know with the initial commission and I'll do it.
  • Unity project setups assume that we are starting from just a model, otherwise it is a flat $10 for PC + Quest.

Other notes:

  • I also do clothing refits, though as I am still in the early stages of learning this results may not be as desired.
  • I do avatar edits as well, and the same applies as to refits. I'm still learning. Though I'm always willing to give a go at something to see if I can do it.
  • As such with these items, prices on these are negotiable. Keep in mind that I am still learning and these may make the commission take longer than a re-texture timeframe.

My Terms of Service

  • You will need to provide proof of ownership of any non-free assets or bases you would like me to use.
  • There is not a whole lot that I won't do, provided it is not against an asset or avatar's own terms of service.
  • All base (starting) prices are final.
  • I will not spring additional costs on you without telling you, and will run it by you before doing anything.
  • I won't do heavily NSFW content, but if you have a request for that feel free to ask anyways, just know I may not be able to do it.
  • If you are not happy with the result(s) please let me know and I will try my best to make it right where I can..

Payment:

  • I take paypal only, as it works out for both of us in the end should anything happen.
  • Payment works in a 50/50 type deal. 50% of the initial cost once everything is finalised, then the other 50% upon completion.
  • Upon the second half of payment, I will then send the completed commission over, and if needed work with you to upload it.
  • HOWEVER: If you do not pay, I reserve the right to: If it is a retexture, sell the retexture on Gumroad, or make it public in my commission world.. If it an avatar, it will be made public, and uploaded to my commission world.
